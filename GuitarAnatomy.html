GuitarExplore

Headstock: Holds the tuning pegs and keeps the strings in place.
Nut: Guides the strings from the headstock to the fretboard.
Neck & Fretboard: Where you press the strings to play notes and chords.
Body: The main part of the guitar, which amplifies the sound.
Bridge: Anchors the strings to the body and transfers vibration.
Pickups: Convert string vibrations into electrical signals (for electric guitars).
